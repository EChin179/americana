Medical Resources

Alzheimer’s Disease Clinical Trials

Syrentis is a clinical research institute which conducts clinical trials with an emphasis in CNS disorders (Alzheimer's Dementia, Major Depressive Disorder, Bipolar Disorder, Anxiety Disorder, Insomnia) in addition to several other disorders, and new drug delivery systems.

Alzheimer’s Orange County

Orange County’s center for Alzheimer’s resources, dedicated to providing quality care and support services to those who are affected by Alzheimer’s disease or other related dementia, and also partnering with local researchers to populate clinical trials to help find a cure.

Alzheimer’s Association

The Alzheimer's Association is the leading voluntary health organization in Alzheimer's care, support and research. Our mission is to eliminate Alzheimer's disease through the advancement of research; to provide and enhance care and support for all affected; and to reduce the risk of dementia through the promotion of brain health.

National Institute on Aging

The National Institutes of Health (NIH), a part of the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services, is the nation’s medical research agency — making important discoveries that improve health and save lives.

Back in Motion Physical Therapy

Back In Motion has developed a unique approach to physical therapy, creating a comprehensive "hands on" program tailoring a customized treatment plan based on your specific needs.

24hr Home Care Company

24 Hour Home Care® has been impacting lives by providing high quality in-home care for over 10 years, specializing in diagnosis-specific care that improves our clients' quality of life.

Council on Aging

The Council on Aging – Southern California has been a trusted nonprofit organization since 1973, providing unbiased information, programs, and services to seniors and disabled adults across Orange, Riverside, San Bernardino, Inyo and Mono counties.

Metlife

MetLife Foundation is working to keep older adults connected and productive, providing them with the support they need to share their experience and expertise with the community.
