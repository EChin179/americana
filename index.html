Our Mission

We are committed to providing our members with an improved quality of life, promoting dignity and independence in the most stimulating and enjoyable manner possible.

Included in Every
Americana Day

Americana Senior Care is an innovatively designed senior day program in Tustin, CA, that offers members personalized care to meet individual needs.

Happiness is Our Priority

We love our members, and we want to make them experience the comfort of home with the fun of a vacation resort. Join our community today.

Community Activities

Socialization

Healthy social connections are proven to combat isolation and depression. We offer the opportunity for members to develop friendships and enjoy time together in meals and activities.
