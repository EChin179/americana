Our Staff

Senior Care Facility Staff Spotlight

Crissie Becerril

Program Manager

Crissie is a Certified Activity Coordinator who is compassionate, bubbly and outgoing. She motivates our members with her cheerfulness and activities that promote social interaction, reality orientation and intellectual stimulation.

Ariel Flores

Social Services Coordinator

Ariel is currently pursuing her Master’s of Science in Gerontology. For the past three years, she has been working with Administration in different Assisted Living, Skilled Nursing, and senior care facilities. Ariel is passionate in her work in the senior care industry and loves working with seniors.

Viridiana De La Rosa

Program Assistant

Viridiana likes to go by Viri for short. When she was 18, she followed her sister's footsteps in homecare and went into caregiving. There she grew her passion for the elderly and enjoys doing activities with them. During her free time she enjoys horseback riding. Viri's goal is to be an LVN one day.

Jocelyn Jara

Program Assistant

Jocelyn likes to go by “JJ” for short. JJ has her Bachelors of Science in Human Services with an emphasis in Gerontology from CSUF. She has been in the senior care industry since 2014. Her goal is to become a program director one day. In her free time, she enjoys going to Disneyland!

Darryl Golliday

Kitchen Supervisor

Darryl has been in the food service field for more than 35 years and is the person instrumental for the delicious meals served at Americana. He is so popular with our members that he was nominated and was a finalist for the 2017 Senior Care Hero Award.
