Programs

Memory Care

Professional Alzheimer’s and Dementia Care
Tustin, Irvine, and Orange County, CA

Do you have a loved one who suffers from dementia or Alzheimer's? Americana Senior Care wants to help your loved one stay engaged and socializing with our dementia care program. As a professional and private memory and Alzheimer's care facility in Tustin, CA, we have the experience and the compassion to give your loved one the attention and care they need.

In addition, we offer three meals a day - with snacks in between - from our own kitchen staffed with our hired cooks. A professional dietician plans all three meals based on what our seniors love and need.

Each memory care senior receives personalized attention from our staff to ensure that we meet all your loved one's needs. We provide WanderGuard equipment as well to keep your loved one safe from harm.

Why We're a Good Alternate Senior Care Choice

We help seniors who don't need 24-hour assisted living care while providing the socialization that seniors on home care don't often receive. We are a private program, which means that we provide a different level of care than other public alternatives. However, we don't charge expensive rates that will break a family caregiver's or a senior's budget. We are fully licensed by the California Department of Social Services, which means that we adhere to strict regulations concerning staffing, location, and admittance.

We understand that you want the best for your loved one who has dementia or Alzheimer's. Call us at (714) 408-2081 to ask questions or schedule your tour today.

Wellness Program

Salon & Spa at Americana

On the second floor of our building is Salon & Spa at Americana, which offers Americana Senior Care members and their families a holistic approach toward enhancing their quality of life. Americana staff can schedule appointments and escort members to and from these services.

Treat yourself to beauty and relaxation in the hands of our attentive and skilled hair stylists, nail technicians, estheticians and therapists. We are open to the public and offer excellent services at affordable prices in an attractive ambience that is clean, modern and cheerful.

Americana members get 20% discount from listed prices!
